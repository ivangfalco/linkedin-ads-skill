# Full-Funnel LinkedIn Ads Framework

Unified framework for running LinkedIn Ads across the entire buyer journey. All proven approaches align on the core structure - differences are in naming and granularity, not direction.

## Core Principle

Only ~5% of your B2B market is "in-market" at any given time. 90% of those prefer vendors they already know. The full funnel exists to build trust with the 95% so when they're ready, you're the obvious choice.

## The 3-Layer Model

All approaches agree on this structure. Names vary (TOF/MOF/BOF, Awareness/Consideration/Conversion, Create/Capture/Accelerate) but the concept is identical.

### Layer 1: TOF - Cold Engagement

**Purpose:** Get on the radar. Build retargeting audiences. NOT meant to drive direct conversions.

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Objective | Engagement, Brand Awareness, Video Views, Website Visits |
| Audience | Cold - Job Title + Industry + Company Size (exclude competitors & customers) |
| Audience size | 50K-300K (general). 60K-400K also works. If total addressable < 30K, skip funnel split |
| Ad types | TLAs on Engagement or Brand Awareness (highest priority), Video Ads (10-30s), Single Image |
| Ad content | Educational, pain-point focused, thought leadership - NOT product pitches |
| CTA | Learn More |
| Landing page | Homepage or content hub |
| Budget share | 30-50% of total (30-40% is standard, lean 50% early on) |
| Key metric | Impressions, engagement rate, video view rate, retargeting pool growth |

**Key actions:**
- Launch broad awareness campaigns with educational content + product intro videos
- Use TLAs from founder/executives - they outperform company page ads 3-6x in CTR
- Optimize creative for engagement and click-through
- Route clicks to content hubs or homepage

### Layer 2: MOF - Retargeting / Nurture

**Purpose:** Build trust with people who already engaged. Prove value through social proof, case studies, deeper content.

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Objective | Website Visits, Video Views |
| Audience | Retargeting - video viewers (50%+) + website visitors + ad engagers from TOF |
| Audience size | 1K-30K (warm audiences are naturally smaller) |
| Ad types | VTLAs, TLAs (Website Visits objective), Case Study Ads, Single Image with deeper product benefits, Document Ads |
| Ad content | Case studies, testimonials, product demos, free templates/resources, feature education |
| CTA | Learn More, Read More |
| Landing page | Solution pages, case study pages, feature-specific pages |
| Budget share | 18-25% of total |
| Key metric | CTR, cost per engagement, content consumption |

**Key actions:**
- Use data-driven retargeting to reach prior engagers with practical resources
- Segment campaigns for personalization by job title, industry, company size
- Rotate 4-6 creatives to prevent fatigue
- Consider Text Ads + Spotlight Ads for low-cost brand reinforcement (Spotlight CPM ~$5.23 vs $20+ for single image)
- Guide users to content or feature-specific landing pages

**Retargeting windows:**
- 90-day nurture layer: educational assets, authority content
- 30-day high-intent layer: strong CTAs
- 180-day low-cost sustainer: text, spotlight, follower ads

### Layer 3: BOF - Conversion

**Purpose:** Drive form submissions, demo requests, sign-ups from warmest prospects.

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Objective | Website Visits (conversions), Lead Gen Forms |
| Audience | High-intent retargeting - video viewers (97%+), website visitors (important pages), ad engagers |
| Audience size | 1K-5K (smallest, highest intent) |
| Ad types | TLAs on Website Visits or Lead Gen objective with strong CTA, Single Image, Lead Gen Forms, Social Proof (G2 ratings, client logos) |
| Ad content | Direct CTA (book demo, try free, request pricing), customer testimonials, awards, specific results |
| CTA | Try Now, Request Demo, Try for Free, Start Free Trial, Book a Demo |
| Landing page | Dedicated demo or free trial landing page |
| Budget share | 20-30% of total |
| Key metric | Cost per conversion, cost per form submission, conversion rate |

**Key actions:**
- Retarget high-intent audiences with testimonials, awards, logo clusters
- Use strong CTAs and minimize friction on landing pages
- Highlight specific results (e.g., "88% sign-up lift", "350% revenue growth")
- Consider Lead Gen Forms for lower friction than website forms
- Document ads + Lead Gen Forms for demo page visitors can yield 2.5x higher demo-to-close rate

## Advanced: 5-Stage Demand Engine

The 5-Stage Demand Engine extends the 3-layer model with two additional stages plus a more granular awareness mapping.

| Stage | Awareness Level | Budget % | Description |
|-------|----------------|----------|-------------|
| **Create** | Unaware | 19% | Build brand affinity and trust with people who don't know you exist |
| **Create** | Problem Aware | 19% | Validate their pain, introduce your category |
| **Capture** | Solution Aware | 10% | Convert interest from in-market buyers comparing solutions |
| **Capture** | Product Aware | 38% | Retarget with proof, demos, strong CTAs - this is where most conversion budget goes |
| **Accelerate/Activate** | Offer Aware | 10% | Accelerate = help close deals faster. Activate = convert free users to paid |
| **Revive** | Offer Aware | 2% | Restart closed-lost opportunities with incentivized campaigns (free audit, exclusive report) |
| **Expand** | Existing Customer | 2% | Upsell/cross-sell, feature announcements, case studies from similar companies |

**Key insight:** Product Aware retargeting (38%) gets the single biggest budget allocation - larger than either TOF stage. This reflects the principle that once someone knows you, the highest-ROI move is converting that awareness into pipeline.

**Campaign naming convention:** `[Region] - [Stage] - [Awareness] - [Type] - {Detail}`
Examples:
- `NA - Capture - Solution Aware - Prospecting - {Persona}`
- `NA - Capture - Product Aware - Remarketing (Offers)`
- `NA - Activate - Offer Aware - Remarketing (Free Users)`
- `NA - Revive - Offer Aware - Remarketing (Closed Lost)`

## Progressive Timeline (6-Month Playbook)

| Month | Focus | Actions |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Foundation | Cold awareness campaigns - pain points + core offer. Set up all retargeting audiences. |
| 2-3 | Trust building | Launch retargeting with case studies, testimonials. Start nurture layer. |
| 4 | Refinement | Use 90-day performance data to refine audiences. Cut underperformers. Double down on winners. |
| 5 | Conversion push | Add high-intent CTAs at BOF. Introduce Lead Gen Forms. |
| 6 | Scale | Niche scaling with 180-day nurture layers. Launch ABM if applicable. Test new audience segments. |

## Budget Framework by Spend Level

| Monthly Budget | Recommended Structure |
|---------------|----------------------|
| $500-$1K | 1-2 campaigns only. Focus on highest-intent audience. Skip TOF, go straight to retargeting if you have existing traffic. |
| $1K-$2.5K | 2-3 campaigns. Light TOF + focused BOF. One TLA campaign + one retargeting campaign. |
| $2.5K-$5K | Full 3-layer funnel. 3-5 campaigns. Start with TOF heavy, shift budget to BOF as retargeting pool grows. |
| $5K-$10K | Full funnel + creative testing. 5-7 campaigns. Multiple TLA variants. Test ad formats. Weekly optimization. |
| $10K-$20K | Full funnel + niche campaigns + ABM. 7-10+ campaigns. Segment by industry verticals. Test Conversation Ads. Add Revive/Expand stages. |

## Creative Hierarchy by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Creative Priority |
|-------|------------------|
| Cold (TOF) | Simple pain-point positioning. Educational. "Did you know...?" or "The problem with X is..." |
| Retargeting (MOF) | Document ads, webinar clips, case stories, playbooks, free resources |
| Conversion (BOF) | ROI data, competitive comparisons, testimonials, pricing previews, strong CTAs |

## 9 Campaign Objectives on LinkedIn

Not all objectives are relevant for B2B SaaS. Here's when to use each:

| Objective | B2B SaaS Relevance | Funnel Stage |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Brand Awareness | Medium - use for initial market entry or brand campaigns | TOF |
| Website Visits | High - drives traffic to content and landing pages | TOF/MOF |
| Video Views | High - builds retargeting pools efficiently | TOF |
| Engagement | High - amplifies content, builds social proof | TOF |
| Lead Generation | High - on-platform forms, lower friction | BOF |
| Website Conversions | High - tracks off-platform conversion events | BOF |
| Talent Leads | Low - recruiting only | N/A |
| Job Applicants | Low - recruiting only | N/A |

**Key rule:** The objective you pick determines how LinkedIn's algorithm optimizes delivery. Don't optimize for engagement if you want demos - and vice versa.

## Universal Settings (All Sources Agree)

- Turn OFF Audience Expansion - always
- Turn OFF LinkedIn Audience Network - always
- Exclude competitors and existing customers from cold campaigns
- Exclude unpaid, training, and entry-level seniority
- Use AND narrowing (not OR) for audience targeting
- Don't use age, gender, or language targeting - LinkedIn has limited data on these
