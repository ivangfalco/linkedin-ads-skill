# B2B SaaS LinkedIn Ads Benchmarks

## CTR Benchmarks by Funnel Stage (200+ B2B SaaS accounts)

**IMPORTANT: CTR terminology varies by campaign objective.** For Engagement objective campaigns (including TLAs), LinkedIn's "CTR" metric is actually **engagement rate** (reactions, comments, post clicks) not click-through-rate to landing page. **CTRTLP** (CTR to landing page) is the real CTR to off-platform destinations. The benchmarks below (0.30%-1.30%) apply to **CTRTLP**, not to the "CTR" column reported for Engagement campaigns.

| Audience Type | CTRTLP Range | Notes |
|--------------|-----------|-------|
| Cold (TOF) | 0.30% - 0.55% | Anything above 0.44% is above platform average. For Engagement campaigns, compare engagement rate, not post CTR |
| Warm / Retargeting (MOF) | 0.55% - 0.80% | Higher because audience already knows you |
| Engaged / Converted (BOF) | 0.80% - 1.30% | Highest intent, highest CTR to landing page |

**Reality check:** 0.45% is the overall LinkedIn Ads average CTRTLP. Anything above 1% is genuinely good. Above 1.5% for Sponsored Content is strong. Message Ads should target >1.5% CTRTLP.

## Cost Per Conversion / Lead Benchmarks

| Metric | Range | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Cost per engagement | $2 - $5 | Below $3 is strong |
| Cost per conversion (on-platform) | $15 - $50 | Varies heavily by industry and offer |
| Cost per lead (Lead Gen Form) | $50 - $200 | Depends on offer value and audience |

## Form Fill Rate

- Target: above 8%
- Below 8% = form is too long, offer isn't compelling enough, or audience isn't warm enough

## TLA-Specific Benchmarks

| Metric | TLA Performance | Standard Ad Performance |
|--------|----------------|------------------------|
| CTR | 1.7x higher | Baseline |
| Engagement rate | 1.6x higher | Baseline |
| CPC | As low as $4.14 | $8-$22+ |
| Company page ads comparison | 3-6x higher CTR | Baseline |

**Why TLAs win:** Ads from real executives feel organic in the feed. They build trust. They don't look like ads. LinkedIn's algorithm favors them because they drive genuine engagement.

## ROAS Benchmarks

- B2B SaaS average ROAS on LinkedIn: ~113%
- This is a lagging metric - LinkedIn Ads impact is often seen 3-6 months after engagement
- Pipeline attribution is more meaningful than last-click ROAS for LinkedIn

## Key Ratios to Monitor

| Ratio | What It Tells You |
|-------|-------------------|
| Engagement rate > 1% | Creative resonates |
| Landing page CTR / Total CTR | How many engagers actually visit your site (often shockingly low) |
| Video view rate (starts/impr) > 40% | Creative hooks attention (horizontal 16:9) |
| Video completion rate (completions/starts) 8-15% | Content holds attention (horizontal 16:9). Sub-3% for vertical |
| Cost per 50% view < $4 | Video cost efficiency. $2-4 avg, $0.21 best case |
| Retargeting pool growth rate | TOF is doing its job |
| BOF conversion rate > 5% | Retargeting audience is warm enough |

## Industry Context

These benchmarks are for B2B SaaS. Adjust expectations for:
- **Enterprise / high ACV:** Higher CPLs are acceptable ($300-$500+) because deal sizes justify it
- **SMB / low ACV:** Need cheaper CPLs ($50-$150) to make unit economics work
- **Healthcare vertical:** Often higher CPMs due to smaller, specialized audiences
- **Tech vertical:** More competitive, benchmark CPCs tend to be at the higher end
