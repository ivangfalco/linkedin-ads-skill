# Prompt Library

Run these once your account is connected (`/onboarding` first). Paste as-is or adapt. Nothing that spends money runs without confirming, and new campaigns and ads are always created paused.

## Reporting
- "Build the 30-day report."
- "Give me the 7, 30, and 90-day report and tell me what changed."
- "Which campaigns are below 0.4% CTR over the last 30 days?"
- "Where am I wasting spend right now?"

## Audits and analysis
- "Audit my account and give me a prioritized fix list."
- "Is Audience Expansion or the Audience Network on anywhere? Flag it."
- "Pull demographics for my top campaign and flag anything off-ICP."

## Campaign and ad creation
- "Create a paused lead-gen campaign for [audience] at $150/day."
- "Create a single-image ad for campaign [id] with this headline and image."
- "Create these five ads in bulk from this CSV."

## Bulk edits
- "Pause every campaign under 0.4% CTR."
- "Add the prefix '[Q2]' to these campaigns and raise their budget to $200/day."
- "Move campaigns 111 and 222 to manual CPC at $5."
- "Add these companies as an exclusion across my prospecting campaigns."
- "Add UTMs (source linkedin, medium paid_social) to my retargeting ads."

## Copywriting
- "Write three headline options for [audience] at the TOF stage, using the formulas."
- "Here's my draft ad copy - audit it for AI slop and rewrite the weak parts."
- "Turn these customer quotes into a stat-interrupt headline."

## Creative
- "Walk me through generating a single-image creative for this campaign."
- "Set up my branding so creatives stay consistent."
- "Generate three creative concepts for this headline, then push the best one as a paused ad."

## Strategy
- "Build a full-funnel plan for $30k/month targeting [ICP]."
- "What's the right TOF/MOF/BOF split for my goal of [X]?"
- "Review my account structure and tell me how to reorganize it."
